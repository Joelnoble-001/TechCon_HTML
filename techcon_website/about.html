Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small gathering of tech enthusiasts to one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Each year, we strive to bring the latest innovations and thought leadership to our attendees.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment where technology professionals can share knowledge, inspire innovation, and build lasting connections. We are committed to promoting diversity and inclusion within the tech community.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a wide array of influential speakers from various sectors of the technology industry. Past speakers include CEOs of leading tech companies, renowned researchers, and pioneering entrepreneurs.
